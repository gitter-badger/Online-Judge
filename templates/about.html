After having some precious conversation, I requested them to assist me in hosting this contest. After huge restrictions...They agreed! Yayy!!
The only aim of this website(online judge) is to inject the flavour of solving and designing algorithmic junk in programming aspirants.

This website has been particularly coded in Erlang, Clojure and Python only to host this contest and the future contests.
Thanks to the founders of those languages!
Contest graph, scoreboard, judge and few other parts of the online judge required employing Graph, Machine Learning and some complex algorithms. Never mind. We love to do it!
There will be six problems of varying difficulty. Read the problem. Code it using your favoite editor and submit it.
'AC' represents 'Accepted'. 'WA' represents 'Wrong Answer'. 'TLE' represents 'Time Limit Error'.

Hopefully after this contest, some 100 problems and few tutorials will be posted on this website. Users can login and solve them to improve their concepts and ratings.
